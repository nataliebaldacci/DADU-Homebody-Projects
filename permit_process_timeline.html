Application Checklist

1 Before You Apply

  • Verify parcel eligibility (USD, R/RS zone)
  • Check for restrictive covenants on title
  • Confirm lot size for max DADU size
  • Review setback requirements

2 Required Documents

  • Recorded restrictive covenant
  • Site plan showing DADU location
  • Building plans and elevations
  • Proof of ownership

3 During Review

  • Respond promptly to reviewer comments
  • Submit revisions within 30 days
  • Track application status online
  • Schedule pre-construction meeting

4 After Permit Issued

  • Post permit on job site
  • Schedule required inspections
  • Obtain Certificate of Occupancy
  • Update property records

Tips to Avoid Delays

Submit complete applications with all required documents

Respond to reviewer comments within 48 hours

Record covenant BEFORE submitting permit application
