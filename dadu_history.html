Nashville DADU History

Timeline of Legislative Changes

Nashville has gradually expanded accessory dwelling unit permissions over two decades. The December 2025 reform represents the most significant change, allowing DADUs by-right in the Urban Services District for the first time.

2005
BL2005-650 ↗
Accessory Building Size Increase
Increased maximum accessory building size from 600 SF to 700 SF, providing slightly more flexibility for detached structures.
2011
BL2011-900 ↗
DADU Definition Created
Added "Detached Accessory Dwelling Unit" as a formal land use category in the zoning code, distinguishing it from other accessory structures.
2017
BL2017-608 ↗
Expanded Zoning Districts
Permitted DADUs in additional zoning districts, expanding eligibility beyond the original limited areas.
2021
BL2021-620 ↗
DADU Overlay District Created
Established the DADU Overlay District, allowing neighborhoods to petition for DADU eligibility through a formal rezoning process.
Current Law
2025
BL2025-1007 ↗
By-Right in Urban Services District
DADUs now permitted by-right in R and RS zones within the Urban Services District. No overlay or special permit required. Effective December 12, 2025.
