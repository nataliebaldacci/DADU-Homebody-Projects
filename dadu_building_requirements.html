Nashville DADU Building Requirements

BL2025-1007 ¬∑ Effective December 12, 2025
DADU Eligibility Requirements
  • Parcel zoning must permit DADUs with conditions (PC) per the Land Use Table
  • Lot must meet minimum lot area for its zoning district (see 17.12)
  • Urban Services District: By-right in R or RS zones
  • General Services District: Requires DADU overlay, UDO, or SP zoning
  • Only one DADU per parcel; must have one single-family principal structure
  • Cannot build DADU if two or more principal structures exist on lot

Additional Restrictions
DADU cannot be built over an easement, platted setback, or side street setback on corner lots.
Use Restrictions
Owner Occupancy
Owner must live in either the primary residence OR the DADU. Both units must have same owner.
Restrictive Covenant
Must be filed with Davidson County Register of Deeds before permit is issued.
Subordinate Structure
DADU must be clearly subordinate in size, height, and purpose to the principal structure.
No Short-Term Rentals
STRPs not permitted in DADUs on single-family, RN, or RL zoned lots.
Design Standards
Style Matching
Similar style, design, material color, roof form, and pitch as principal structure.
Dormer Rules
May cover no more than 50% of roof. Must be set back 2 ft from exterior wall.
Other Accessory Structures
No other accessory structure can exceed 200 SF when a DADU exists on lot.
Location
DADU must be located to the rear of the principal structure.

Additional Requirements Apply

  • Setbacks, size limitations, and design requirements enforced by Historic Commission

  • Contact Metropolitan Historic Zoning Commission for design guidelines
How to Apply for a Permit
1
Hire a Contractor
Property owners cannot pull self-permits for DADU construction. A registered contractor must submit the application.
2
Prepare Documents
Site plan with setbacks, elevation drawings, floor plans, and recorded restrictive covenant.
3
Submit to Zoning Help Desk
Application assigned to zoning examiner for review. Use ePermits portal to track progress.
4
Complete Checklist
Obtain all required signoffs from Codes divisions and Metro agencies before permit issuance.
5
Pay and Build
Once approved, pay permit fees and begin construction. Schedule required inspections with Metro Nashville.
